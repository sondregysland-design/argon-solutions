# Argon Solutions: Custom Software for Oil and Gas in Stavanger, Norway

Argon Solutions is a Norwegian software company headquartered in Stavanger, Norway, the oil and energy capital of Scandinavia. Founded in 2024 by Sondre Gysland, an engineer with hands-on experience from offshore operations and subsea projects on the Norwegian Continental Shelf, the company builds custom software solutions tailored to the unique challenges of the energy industry.

## What Argon Solutions Does

Argon Solutions develops four categories of technology solutions for the oil and gas sector:

### 1. Custom Software Development
Web applications, dashboards, and project management tools built with Next.js, React, TypeScript, and Supabase. Notable projects include Argon Prosjektstyring, an offshore project management platform with role-based access control and ROV system tracking modules.

### 2. CRM and Sales Automation
A complete CRM system (Argon CRM) with automated prospecting from 6 lead sources, AI-driven lead scoring powered by Claude AI (Anthropic), PandaDoc integration for proposal generation, and daily automation sweeps that capture, enrich, and score new leads.

### 3. System Integration
API development, AI-powered report generation, and automated data flow between platforms. Argon Solutions connects existing systems and builds custom integrations that eliminate manual data transfer.

### 4. AI Agents and Browser Automation
Intelligent agents built with Playwright that automate browser tasks, data extraction, and repetitive processes. One project reduced 6 hours of weekly manual portal work to 3 minutes by automating certificate downloads from 47 supplier portals.

## Products

Argon Solutions offers five software products:

- **Argon Prosjektstyring**: Offshore project management with ROV tracking, procedures, tasks, and inventory management
- **Argon CRM**: Customer relationship management with 6 lead sources, AI scoring, and automated follow-up
- **Argon Dashboard**: Project board with data import, real-time sync, and progress tracking
- **Argon Docs**: AI-powered PDF extraction for invoices, safety documents, and certificates using Claude AI
- **Argon RSA**: Risk management with HAZOP, HAZID, and bow-tie analysis

## Technology Stack

Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Supabase, PostgreSQL, Claude AI (Anthropic), Playwright, Vercel, Node.js
